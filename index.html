Who We Are

Built on Rhetoric.
Driven by Truth.

BMSC Debate Club is a prestigious student society dedicated to the art and science of formal debate. We train future advocates, policymakers, and intellectuals through weekly workshops, inter-university tournaments, and mentorship from seasoned professionals.

Our alumni have gone on to careers at the United Nations, Supreme Courts, and Fortune 500 boardrooms. The skills you build here echo for a lifetime.
